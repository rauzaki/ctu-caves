Director

Jonathan Maglasang, Ph.D.

Professor, Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering

Email: jonathan.maglasang@ctu.edu.ph

Phone: (+63) 917-719-7694

Office: ENG 401, New Academic Bldg, Cebu Technological University - Main Campus

Dr. Jonathan Maglasang is a Professor of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering and a University Director at Cebu Technological University (CTU). He is the coordinator for the BS Aerospace Engineering (BSASE) program and the director of the Center for Advanced Vehicles and Energy Systems. He is also engaged in promoting CTU as a national and international research collaborating hub in Central Philippines.
Dr. Jonathan Maglasang has advised and mentored several undergraduates, master’s, and doctoral students in their research work. He obtained his MS and Ph.D. in Aeronautics & Astronautics (Aerospace Engineering) from Kyushu University and his BS in Mechanical Engineering degree from MSU-IIT.
His research and development interests include performance enhancements of renewable energy systems, wind, gas, and hydraulic turbines; aerial, terrain, and underwater robotics; flapping, rotary, and fixed-wing unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs); wing-in-surface effect (WISE) vehicles for rapid and efficient inter-island transportation; hybrid rocket propulsion systems; indigenous rocket propulsion; biomimetics; and applied aeronautics and astronautics. He has R&D projects funded by the Department of Science and Technology (DOST).
He loves fishing, farming, 3D printing, mental/thought experiments, and unconventional designing. He establishes his Design, Research, Engineering, Analysis, and Manufacturing (DREAM) Laboratory at home with his kids as his research assistants and lab rats. He is a silent type, and if he is not reading or talking, he is probably running some iterative mental calculations and experiments. And most of all, he is of God and family.
